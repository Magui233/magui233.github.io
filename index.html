Michael Aguirre

Influenced Culture Media, CEO

Growing up in big family of five, I was faced with many unforeseen tragic challenges unfortunately by the sudden passing of my father at the tender age of seven. Resources were cut tremendously short by this but, brought my family closer together as the man upstairs, Jesus Christ guided us through the storm. My siblings, mother and I were stunned more than anything, given I was so young I had no idea of the repercusions but the Lord ensued trust and faith is us all as we navigated through the times. This tragedy only got my creative juices to start flowing as I was forced to amuse myself by any means necessary. This was great as my indigenous native blood did as they did when living with what was essentially only available to them. This idea only sparked my artistic prowls as it got me intuitively thinking of how I was going to survive this life.

Thankfully, I found myself my first job at the YMCA where I started off handing out towels doing the entry level position but, by the time I departed I was the Human Resources and Marketing execute holding multiple positions as the exploits were just crazy. Given my lack of education, age and unknowingness of the companies exploits as I was just promoting to higher positions I went along for the ride gaining the knowledge and experience. I gained business office professional skills and also artistic abilities first hand as I engulfed myself. Eventually when the company wasn't doing well I was forced to lay myself off and pursue other ventures. I landed other marketing and business positions and was given the opportunity to continue growing before the pandemic of 2020 halted everything. Right before the nationwide shut down started I created this business venture called Influenced Culture Media as an avenue for all types of arts to be created. Unfortunately, in 2022 while building my business I suffered a near fatal car accident but, miraculously did not perish as the Lord saved my life and has given me another chance. With serious traumatic brain, skull, and many other ailments I've sustained 15 major surgeries so far but, on the bright side have gotten back into education and still going for glory.

Today we see these crazy ideas are coming to real life as I restarted my educational journey and am attending the University of California, Riverside perusing my dreams. Some day this idea the "anything is possible" will be when Influenced Culture Media creates yours dreams, Lord willing
